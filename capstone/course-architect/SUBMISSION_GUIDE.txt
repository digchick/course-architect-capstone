==========================================================================
  PART B — RECORD THE VIDEO  (easiest first)
==========================================================================
Open VIDEO_SCRIPT.md and keep it on screen to read from. Then:

OPTION 1 — Clipchamp (already on Windows 11, records the whole screen + your voice)
  1. Click Start, type "Clipchamp", open it (sign in / skip if asked).
  2. Click "Record something" -> "Screen recording" (camera icon on the left).
  3. Turn the microphone ON (so it records your voice). Webcam is optional.
  4. Click the red Record button -> choose "Entire screen" -> Share.
  5. Now do the demo while reading the [SAY] lines from VIDEO_SCRIPT.md:
        - double-click "Describe a Course.bat", type an Excel topic, press Enter
        - when "My Course.pptx" opens, scroll through the slides, show the Notes
        - talk over the cover image and the architecture/coverage points
  6. Click Stop. Clipchamp drops the clip onto its timeline.
  7. (Optional) trim the start/end by dragging the clip edges.
  8. Click "Export" (top right) -> 1080p -> it saves an .mp4 to your Downloads.
  >>> Keep it UNDER 5 minutes. About 4 is perfect. Practice once first.

OPTION 2 — Xbox Game Bar (quickest, but records ONE app window, not File Explorer)
  - Press Win + G, click the Record (circle) button, do your demo inside ONE
    window (e.g. PowerPoint), press Win + Alt + R to stop. The clip saves to
    Videos\Captures. Good if you only want to narrate over the finished deck.

TIP: If a live build makes you nervous, just open one of the finished decks
(course_gemini.pptx or Excel Course.pptx) and TALK OVER IT. Showing the real
slides + explaining how the agent made them is a perfectly good video.
