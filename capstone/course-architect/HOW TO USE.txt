HOW TO MAKE A COURSE  -  Course Architect
==========================================

You describe what you want. The AI designs the outline AND writes the whole course.


EASIEST WAY  -  just describe it
-------------------------------
1. Double-click  "Describe a Course.bat"
2. Type what you want, for example:
      How to write basic Excel formulas for office staff
3. Press Enter. The agent designs the course and opens it as "My Course.pptx".


OR  -  if you want to control the outline yourself
--------------------------------------------------
1. Open  "MY_COURSE.md"  in Notepad.
2. Either:
     (a) write ONE line = your topic, e.g.   Customer service for new staff
         (the AI designs the whole outline for you), OR
     (b) write the outline yourself:
            # Course Title
            Audience: Who it is for
            ## A section heading
            - a point to teach
            - another point
3. Save it, then double-click  "Make My Course.bat".

Your finished deck opens as  "My Course.pptx".
(Close that file before rebuilding, or it can't save over it.)

------------------------------------------------------------------
Notes
- Real AI content uses the Gemini key (already set up in .env).
  On the free tier the default model allows ~80 courses a day - no
  billing needed for normal use.
- With no key it still runs, using simpler built-in text.
